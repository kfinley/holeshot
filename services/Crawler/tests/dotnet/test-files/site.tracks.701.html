Location

Contact Information

  • Email: [email protected]
  • Track Phone: 803-329-5620
  • Primary Contact: Mike King (803-325-2630)
  • Secondary Contact: Matt Beynon (803-326-2436)

The Rock Hill BMX Supercross Track in Rock Hill, South Carolina, is home to the Carolina Nationals and the 2024 UCI BMX World Championships, a dual purpose facility with an 8-meter elite supercross start hill and a 5-meter amateur start hill. It was the first Olympic caliber training facility on the East Coast and one of now five in the United States. The site will host local, state, regional, national USA BMX events and UCI international events while providing a safe introduction to local residents and Olympic hopefuls. The track will be accessible to all ages and skill levels through a variety of programs, races, and events.
